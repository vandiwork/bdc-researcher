Mark Delta — Divergent Valuations (Loan-Level)

Debt positions held by 2+ BDCs where the fair-value price (FV ÷ par) differs most. Matched on borrower + seniority; each fund's price is FV-weighted across its tranches. The headline spread is computed only between material holders (≥ $2M) — a tiny unfunded revolver at one fund won't manufacture a false gap against another's funded term loan; smaller holders still appear in the detail.
