Mark Delta — Divergent Valuations (Loan-Level)

Debt positions held by 2+ BDCs where the fair-value price (FV ÷ par) differs most. To be sure we're comparing the same loan, holders must match on borrower, seniority, maturity year and credit spread — a borrower's 2027 loan at S+5 is never compared against its 2031 loan at S+6. Each fund's price is FV-weighted across that facility's tranches; positions with no extracted par, and non-material holders (< $2M) in the headline spread, are excluded so data artefacts can't manufacture a gap. The remaining deltas are genuine co-lender disagreements. Click any row for per-fund detail.
